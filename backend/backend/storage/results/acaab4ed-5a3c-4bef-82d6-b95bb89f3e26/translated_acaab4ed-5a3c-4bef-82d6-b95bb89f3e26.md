Bank of Refusals  
63 examples  
by weeks and types

The bank is used in two ways: the participant scrolls through the page and recognizes themselves — this lowers the barrier for the first refusal.

Week 1 – Tactical Level  
Refusal of specific actions today

Meetings and Calls

2. Did not attend a meeting that could have been handled with a 5-minute phone call.  
3. Declined a call where I was invited to "just listen."  
4. Postponed a meeting with a contractor — there was no specific issue, so the meeting was premature.  
5. Did not agree to be "on call" on a day off.

6. Canceled all meetings before 10 a.m. — this is my working time.

Rejection of beliefs, roles, and behavior models that define behavior.

How to use the bank  
If it is difficult in the morning to understand what is pushing you away, open the bank and find what resonates with you. This is not a to-do list, but a mirror. Once you recognize yourself, you’ll know what is pushing you away.
